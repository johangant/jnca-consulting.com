About us

JNCA started with over a decade of market-leading digital agency operations and experience in the UK and EU. It exists to encourage the creation of meaningful, working software services and processes into teams and organisations to support and enhance their goals and purpose.

Our core principle is that digital development is an exploratory process and investment, in tandem with you, rather than a transactional process to produce a pre-specified outcome.

We deliver pragmatic, personable software development and technical consultancy with clients in public-sector and government teams, third-sector and modern startups. We specialise in complex, content-driven web systems and services, but our focus is on tangible outcomes over specific technologies or tools.

We prefer and promote Agile practices on our projects rather than specific processes or frameworks.
